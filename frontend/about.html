Our Story

Where Elegance Meets Emotion

At Everaura, we specialize in handcrafted jewellery that speaks your story. Our mission is to create timeless pieces that blend heritage, trend, and personalization for every woman who celebrates her uniqueness. From oxidized treasures to anti-tarnish Kundan sets, each design is made with passion and precision for every age and occasion.

We believe that every piece of jewelry should be more than an accessory; it should be an extension of your identity, a marker of your memories, and a celebration of your journey.

Join us in redefining tradition with a modern touch. Because at Everaura, every piece tells a story — yours.

Meet The Founder

"Vibha Wadhwani was born on 10th December in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh — a place where traditions run deep and every detail tells a story. From her earliest years, she felt a pull towards art, culture, and the silent power carried in heirlooms passed through generations."

"For Vibha, jewelry was never just ornamentation — it was emotion woven into metal, hustle etched into design, and a mirror of one’s inner shine. A woman of resilience and relentless passion, she poured her heart into transforming this vision into reality."

"That vision became Everaura: not simply a brand, but a sanctuary for women who seek pieces that resonate with their journeys, their strength, and their quiet elegance. Vibha’s spirit — equal parts dreamer and doer — continues to guide Everaura, ensuring every creation carries a fragment of story, soul, and timeless artistry."

— Vibha Wadhwani
Founder @ EverAura
